    mne.decoding.get_coef¶

    mne.decoding.get_coef(estimator, attr='filters_', inverse_transform=False)[source]¶

    Retrieve the coefficients of an estimator ending with a Linear Model.

    This is typically useful to retrieve “spatial filters” or “spatial patterns” of decoding models [R38].

    Parameters:

    estimator : object | None

    An estimator from scikit-learn.

    attr : str

    The name of the coefficient attribute to retrieve. Typically ‘filters_‘ or ‘patterns_‘. Defaults to ‘filters_‘.

    inverse_transform : bool

    If True, returns the coefficients after inverse transforming them with the transformer steps of the estimator.
    Returns:

    coef : array

    The coefficients.

    References

    [R38](1, 2) Haufe, S., Meinecke, F., Gorgen, K., Dahne, S., Haynes, J.-D., Blankertz, B., & Biessmann, F. (2014). On the interpretation of weight vectors of linear models in multivariate neuroimaging. NeuroImage, 87, 96-110. doi:10.1016/j.neuroimage.2013.10.067.
